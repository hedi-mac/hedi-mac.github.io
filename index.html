Italy, Venice, 2017 Venice is a dream place to visit for art lovers. From monumental Renaissance paintings to famous works of modern art, The City of Water has accumulated masterpieces in many different forms and shapes. Dotted with art galleries, and museums, The Floating City offers enough masterpieces to make anyone with a budding or an established interest in art immerse themselves in it. I also visited Florence the Renaissance birthplace and one of the cities with the richest history in the world.
Spain, Madrid, 2020 The EU gives promotion major students a scholarship to encourage students to study abroad. This combined with my ongoing fascination with Spanish culture made living abroad an easy choice for me. Madrid is an art-lovers paradise. Even outside of its museums, art is everywhere and sculptures grace parks throughout the city.
